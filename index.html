Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How much do your services cost? +

Our pricing varies based on your specific needs and project scope. We offer packages starting from basic landing pages to complete digital transformation solutions. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your business goals and budget.

How long does it take to build a website? +

A simple landing page takes 1-2 weeks, while a multi-page business website typically takes 3-4 weeks. Custom web applications or e-commerce stores may take 6-8 weeks. Timeline depends on complexity and your feedback speed.

Can you only handle marketing, not development? +

Absolutely! We offer standalone marketing services including social media management, paid ads, SEO, and content creation. You don't need to use our development services to work with us on marketing.

Do you work with clients outside India? +

Yes! While we're based in India, we work with clients globally. We're experienced in managing different time zones and have successfully delivered projects for clients in the US, UK, Australia, and Middle East.

How do we start working together? +

Simple! Book a free consultation call with us. We'll discuss your goals, challenges, and budget. Then we'll create a custom proposal. Once approved, we start with a discovery phase and kick off your project within days.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work? +

We offer unlimited revisions during the project phase to ensure you're 100% satisfied. We work closely with you throughout the process and make adjustments based on your feedback. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion? +

Yes! All our packages include post-launch support. We also offer monthly maintenance and support plans for ongoing updates, security, and optimization. We're here for the long term, not just the launch.
